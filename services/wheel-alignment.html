Wheel Alignment

Using Hunter 3D laser systems for perfect alignment and stability.

Overview

Precise wheel alignment is essential for steering accuracy, even tyre wear, and overall vehicle stability. We use Hunter 3D laser alignment systems, officially approved by Mercedes-Benz, to perform accurate adjustments on all models, including AMG and 4MATIC variants.

What's Included

  • Four-wheel laser alignment
  • Steering angle sensor recalibration
  • Pre- and post-alignment reports

Our Process

  1. Inspection & Measurement: We conduct a visual inspection and measure the current wheel alignment angles.
  2. Platform Mounting: Your vehicle is mounted on our alignment platform with advanced 3D laser sensors attached.
  3. Precision Adjustment: Our technicians adjust the toe, camber, and caster angles to meet exact Mercedes-Benz specifications.
  4. Sensor Recalibration: We reset the steering angle sensor to prevent dashboard warning lights and ensure system harmony.
  5. Documentation: You receive a full report showing the alignment results before and after the adjustment.
