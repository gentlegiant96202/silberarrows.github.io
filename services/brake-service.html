Brake Service & Repair

Comprehensive brake solutions to ensure your safety and performance.

Overview

Your Mercedes-Benz's braking system is critical to safety and performance. We conduct comprehensive brake inspections, servicing, and repairs using only genuine Mercedes-Benz parts. Whether it's routine pad replacement or complex hydraulic repairs, we ensure optimal braking performance and peace of mind, without compromising factory standards.

What's Included

  • Brake pad and disc replacement
  • Brake fluid flush and bleed
  • Calliper and sensor inspection
  • Electronic brake system reset

Our Process

  1. Road Test: We begin with a road test to assess brake feel, noise, and response.
  2. Full Inspection: Our technicians perform a full inspection of pads, discs, brake lines, and callipers.
  3. Measurement & Analysis: We measure pad wear and rotor thickness against manufacturer specifications.
  4. Genuine Parts Replacement: We replace components with genuine Mercedes-Benz parts, followed by bleeding and recalibration of the brake system.
  5. Final Safety Test: A final road test and safety check are performed, and you receive a detailed technician report.
