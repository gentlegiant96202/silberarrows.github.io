Suspension & Steering Repair

Restoring the signature comfort and precision of your Mercedes-Benz.

Overview

Suspension comfort and steering precision are hallmarks of a Mercedes-Benz. We specialise in both conventional and advanced systems including AIRMATIC and ABC (Active Body Control), using genuine Mercedes-Benz parts for all repairs and replacements.

We Handle

  • Shock absorber and strut replacement
  • Air suspension leak repair and compressor service
  • Control arm and bushing replacement
  • Steering rack and power steering system repairs

Our Process

  1. Road Test: We conduct a thorough road test to evaluate handling, comfort, and ride quality.
  2. Component Inspection: Our technicians inspect all suspension components and ride height sensors for wear or damage.
  3. Genuine Parts Replacement: Any worn or faulty parts are replaced using only genuine Mercedes-Benz components.
  4. System Calibration: We calibrate electronic suspension systems and perform precise ride height adjustments.
  5. Final Confirmation: A final road test confirms that the vehicle's handling and comfort are fully restored.
