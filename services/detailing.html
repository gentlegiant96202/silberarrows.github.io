Interior & Exterior Detailing

Protect your investment and restore showroom condition.

Overview

Restore your Mercedes-Benz to showroom condition with our professional detailing services. We use premium, vehicle-safe products tailored to Mercedes-Benz interiors, leather, and paint systems. From multi-stage polishing to optional ceramic coatings, our detailing protects your investment and enhances resale value.

What's Included

  • Snow foam wash and paint correction
  • Ceramic coating (optional)
  • Interior shampoo and leather care
  • Engine bay and alloy wheel detailing

Our Process

  1. Decontamination: We begin with a pre-wash and full paintwork decontamination.
  2. Clay Bar Treatment: A clay bar treatment is used to remove embedded contaminants from the paint surface.
  3. Machine Polishing: Our detailers use machine polishing to eliminate swirl marks, light scratches, and other defects.
  4. Interior Deep Clean: The interior receives a deep cleaning and the leather is professionally conditioned.
  5. Protection Application: Finally, we apply paint sealants or optional Gtechniq ceramic protection for long-lasting results.
