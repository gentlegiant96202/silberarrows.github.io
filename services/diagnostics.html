Electrical & Computer Diagnostics

Using the official Mercedes-Benz diagnostic platform to identify and resolve complex issues.

Overview

Mercedes-Benz vehicles feature advanced electronics across all systems. Using XENTRY Diagnosis, the official Mercedes-Benz diagnostic platform, we identify and resolve issues with ECUs, control modules, sensors, and wiring faults.

Diagnostic Capabilities

  • XENTRY Diagnosis system fault scanning
  • ECU resets and software updates
  • Sensor testing and wiring repairs

Our Process

  1. Full Vehicle Scan: We use XENTRY Diagnosis to retrieve all manufacturer-specific fault codes.
  2. Systematic Analysis: Our technicians perform a systematic analysis based on official manufacturer data and test plans.
  3. Component Testing & Repair: We test and repair affected components, including complex wiring harnesses.
  4. Software Updates: We apply necessary software updates and clear the vehicle's fault memory.
  5. Verification & Report: A final verification scan is performed and you receive a detailed diagnostic report.
