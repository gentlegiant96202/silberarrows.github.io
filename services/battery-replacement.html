Battery Testing & Replacement

Ensuring reliable starts and preventing electrical issues.

Overview

Modern Mercedes-Benz models rely heavily on electrical systems. Our battery services ensure reliable starts and prevent electrical issues. We install original Mercedes-Benz batteries and perform full electronic registration with your vehicle's control units.

Services Include

  • Battery load and voltage test
  • Charging system and alternator inspection
  • Battery replacement and ECU registration

Our Process

  1. Assessment: We perform a comprehensive battery and charging system assessment.
  2. Component Inspection: Our technicians inspect all related terminals, grounds, and cables for wear or corrosion.
  3. Removal & Disposal: We handle the professional removal and environmentally safe disposal of the old battery unit.
  4. Installation & Registration: A new, original Mercedes-Benz battery is installed and registered with the vehicle's software.
  5. System Verification: We conduct a final system test to verify the electrical system is ready and performing correctly.
