Engine Repair & Overhaul

Restoring smooth performance, emissions compliance, and long-term reliability.

Overview

We handle everything from minor engine issues to complete overhauls using factory repair methods and genuine parts. Common services include timing component replacement, gasket and seal replacement, and turbocharger diagnosis and replacement, all performed to restore smooth performance, emissions compliance, and long-term reliability.

Common Repairs

  • Timing chain and tensioner replacement
  • Cylinder head gasket and seal replacement
  • Turbocharger diagnosis and replacement
  • Full engine disassembly and rebuilds

Our Process

  1. Diagnostic Testing: We use advanced diagnostics like compression tests, leak-down tests, and oil analysis to pinpoint issues.
  2. Dismantling & Inspection: Affected components are carefully dismantled and inspected against factory specifications.
  3. Genuine Parts & Reassembly: We use only genuine Mercedes-Benz parts for precise reassembly according to factory methods.
  4. Recalibration & Testing: The ECU is recalibrated, and we conduct thorough post-repair leak and performance testing.
  5. Final Road Test: A final road test and detailed engine condition report are provided to certify the repair.
