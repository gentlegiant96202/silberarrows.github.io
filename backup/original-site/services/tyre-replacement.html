Tyre Replacement & Balancing

Ensuring optimal safety, performance, and comfort with manufacturer-approved tyres.

Overview

Proper tyres are crucial for safety, performance, and comfort. We supply and fit Mercedes-Benz-approved tyres, including run-flats and AMG-specific sizes, and calibrate TPMS sensors to factory standards.

What's Included

  • Tyre fitting and wheel balancing
  • TPMS sensor calibration and reprogramming
  • Run-flat and performance tyre options

Our Process

  1. Inspection: We inspect your existing tyres for wear, damage, and age.
  2. Selection & Fitting: We help you select and then professionally fit the appropriate replacement tyres.
  3. Dynamic Balancing: Our technicians perform a dynamic wheel balancing to eliminate vibrations and ensure a smooth ride.
  4. TPMS Calibration: We reset or program the Tyre Pressure Monitoring System (TPMS) sensors as required by the manufacturer.
  5. Final Test Drive: A final test drive confirms correct fitment and optimal performance.
