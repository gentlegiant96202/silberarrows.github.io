Scheduled Maintenance – Service A & B

Official Mercedes-Benz service schedules to maintain optimal performance.

Overview

Regular servicing is essential to retain your vehicle's efficiency, safety, and long-term value. We follow official Mercedes-Benz service schedules using XENTRY Diagnosis and genuine parts to maintain optimal performance and full-service history integrity.

Service A (Minor Service)

  • Engine oil, oil filter, and air filter replacement
  • Check and top-up of all fluids
  • Visual inspection of underbody, engine bay, brakes, and tyres
  • Diagnostics check using XENTRY Diagnosis
  • Maintenance counter reset and full valet

Service B (Major Service)

Includes all Service A items, plus:

  • Dust or combination filter replacement
  • A/C system treatment
  • Wheel rotation and spare wheel or TIREFIT sealant check
  • Additional safety checks and updates

Our Process

  1. Review of Service History: We review your vehicle's history and the factory-recommended schedule.
  2. Factory Specification Tasks: All maintenance tasks are performed to precise factory specifications.
  3. Genuine Parts: We use only Mercedes-Benz genuine oil and parts for all services.
  4. Record Updates: We update your vehicle's digital service records and reset the maintenance indicator.
  5. Technician Report: You receive a detailed report with our findings and any maintenance recommendations.
