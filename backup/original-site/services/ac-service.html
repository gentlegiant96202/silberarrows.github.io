Air Conditioning Service & Repair

Ensuring optimal cabin comfort and air quality.

Overview

A properly functioning A/C system ensures cabin comfort and air quality. We service all Mercedes-Benz refrigerant types (R134a & R1234yf), replacing filters, diagnosing leaks, and restoring performance.

Services Include

  • Refrigerant recovery, refill, and leak detection
  • Compressor, condenser, and evaporator check
  • Cabin filter replacement
  • Disinfection and odour treatment

Our Process

  1. System Test: We start with a system pressure and leak test to identify any immediate issues.
  2. Component Inspection: Our technicians inspect the blower, condenser, and filter for blockages or damage.
  3. Refrigerant Service: We evacuate and then refill the refrigerant to the correct manufacturer specification.
  4. Disinfection: The air ducts and evaporator housing are disinfected to eliminate bacteria and odours.
  5. Performance Verification: A final test verifies the cooling performance and overall system function.
